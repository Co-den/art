10 useful things to learn and practice for your art work

Amos Chukwuemeka Emmanuel

There are 10 useful things an artist can learn in order to be a successful artist.

As artist, we are often told to take every opportunity that comes our way.

Being an artist includes continuously envolving your skills, improving your techniques and pushing yourself beyond the next boundar. It's part of your growth as an artist and can even see you changing your artistic techniques completely several times during a lifetime.

  • Drawing is key.
  • Great art takes hard work.
  • Use a reference image.
  • Rejection comes to everone.
  • Uniqueness is equal to succes.
  • Nothing is set in stone.
  • Honesty connects people.
  • Start projects right away.
  • Learning never ends always keep at it.
  • Everyone benefits by helping others.

These are the things to know and learn on your journe to becoming a great artist.

How to become a better artist.

This general step-by-step method will provide a comprehensive guide to better our skill as an artist .

  • Start with sketches.
  • Get some good pencils.
  • Select paper.
  • Observe, Observe, Observe.
  • Watch films.
  • Look at silhouettes.
  • Examine other art project.
  • Keep Observing.
  • Practice body forms.
  • Light and value.
  • Do some thumbnails.
  • Rough sketch.
  • Smooth it out.
  • Get creative.
  • Keep practicing.
Don't paint dreams or nightmare, paint your own reality.
